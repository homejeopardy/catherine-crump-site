Leadership

Directing the Samuelson Clinic

As Director of the Samuelson Law, Technology & Public Policy Clinic, Catherine Crump leads students in taking on real cases and policy fights at the frontier of technology and civil liberties — challenging police surveillance, reforming the electronic monitoring of youth, and pressing for public oversight of the tools governments buy.

It is hands-on, public-interest lawyering that trains the next generation while making a difference now.
